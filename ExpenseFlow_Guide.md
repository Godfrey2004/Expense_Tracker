## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **ExpenseFlow**, a premium, offline-first personal finance tracker. ExpenseFlow is designed to help you easily log your daily expenses, monitor your budget, and achieve your saving goals with a beautiful, clutter-free "Sky Blue" interface. 

Because ExpenseFlow is built as a PWA (Progressive Web App), all your data is securely stored **locally on your device**. It works flawlessly without an internet connection.

---

## 2. Installing the App
You do not need to download ExpenseFlow from an App Store. You can install it directly to your phone's home screen for a full-screen app experience.

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the website link in Google Chrome.
2. Tap the **three dots** in the top right corner.
3. Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.
4. Follow the prompt to install. The ExpenseFlow icon will appear on your home screen.

**On iOS (Safari):**
1. Open the website link in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share icon** (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right.

## 3. Home Dashboard
When you open ExpenseFlow, you are greeted by the **Home Dashboard**.
- **Total Balance:** The large number at the top shows your net balance (Income minus Expenses).
- **Income & Expenses:** Quick summaries of your total money in vs. money out.
- **Quick Stats:** Three white cards show how much you've spent **Today**, **This Week**, and **This Month**.
- **Recent Activity:** A quick glance at your latest transactions.

---

## 4. Adding Transactions
Adding an expense or income is quick and easy.

1. Tap the large **Purple "+" Button** at the bottom center of the screen.
2. Select whether it is an **Expense** (money going out) or **Income** (money coming in).
3. **Amount:** Enter the value.
4. **Category:** Choose a category (e.g., Food & Beverage, Transport, Salary).
5. **Date & Time:** By default, it uses right now, but you can change it if you are logging a past purchase. (Dates are formatted as DD/MM/YYYY).
6. **Payment Method & Wallet:** Track exactly where the money came from (e.g., Cash, Credit Card, Main Wallet).
7. Tap **Save Transaction**.

---

## 5. Managing Budgets
Keep your spending in check by setting limits.

1. Tap the **Budget** icon in the bottom navigation menu.
2. Tap **"+ Add"** at the top right.
3. **Select a Category:** Choose which category you want to restrict (e.g., Entertainment).
4. **Set Limit:** Enter the maximum amount you want to spend this month.
5. Tap **Save Budget**.
6. **Monitoring:** The Budget screen will now show a visual progress bar. As you add expenses to that category, the bar will fill up. If you get too close to the limit, it will turn red!

## 6. Analytics & Reports
Understand your spending habits visually.

**Analytics Tab (Bottom Nav):**
- **Monthly Trend:** A chart showing your spending habits over the last 6 months.
- **Category Breakdown:** A pie chart showing exactly which categories eat up most of your money.
- **Top Categories:** A ranked list of your most expensive habits.

**Reports Tab (Side Menu):**
- Tap the **Menu Icon (three lines)** in the top left corner.
- Tap **Reports**.
- Here you can filter your transactions by **Today, Week, Month, Year, or Custom Range**.
- You can easily **Export to CSV** if you want to view your data in Microsoft Excel!

---

## 7. Advanced Features
Open the Side Menu (top left) to access powerful premium tools:

- **Savings Goals:** Set a target (like a New Car or Vacation). The AI will tell you exactly how much you need to save per day to reach it by your deadline!
- **Wallets & Accounts:** Create separate balances for your Bank Account, Cash Wallet, or Credit Card, and transfer money between them.
- **EMI Tracker:** Need to take a loan? Enter the loan amount, interest rate, and months to instantly calculate your monthly EMI and total interest.

---

## 8. Settings & Security
Keep your data safe and personalized. Open the Side Menu and go to **Settings**.

- **App Theme:** Switch between Light Mode, Dark Mode, or System Default.
- **Color Theme:** Change the accent colors of the app.
- **Currency:** Switch between ₹ (INR), $ (USD), € (EUR), or £ (GBP).
- **PIN Security:** Tap "Setup" to create a 4-digit PIN. Every time you open the app, you will need to enter this PIN, keeping your financial data completely private.
- **Backup & Restore:** Because ExpenseFlow is offline, you should occasionally tap **"Backup Data"** to download a save file of your transactions. If you get a new phone, just tap **"Restore Data"** and select that file to get everything back!
