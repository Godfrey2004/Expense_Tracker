## 1. Getting Started
When you open ExpenseFlow for the first time, you are greeted with a beautiful onboarding screen. Once you enter the app, you will land on the **Home Dashboard**.
* **Purpose:** Gives you a quick glance at your current balance, recent spending, and quick actions.
* **How to use it:** Scroll down to see your latest transactions. Tap the floating **"+"** button at the bottom right at any time to log a new expense or income.

---

## 2. Adding Transactions
The core of ExpenseFlow is tracking your money. 
* **Purpose:** Keep an accurate record of where your money comes from and where it goes.
* **How to use it:** 
  1. Tap the big **"+"** button.
  2. Choose **Expense** (money spent) or **Income** (money earned).
  3. Enter the **Amount** and pick a **Category** (like Food, Rent, or Salary).
  4. **Premium Features inside the Transaction Form:**
     * **Smart Tags:** Tap the suggested tags (e.g., `#lunch`, `#uber`) to quickly categorize specific purchases.
     * **Receipt Attachment:** Tap the 📎 **Paperclip** icon to upload a photo of your receipt for safekeeping.
     * **Recurring Expense:** If this is a Netflix subscription or rent, toggle **"Make Recurring"** and set it to Monthly. The app will automatically log this expense for you every month!
     * **Mark Favorite:** Tap the ⭐ star if this is an important transaction you want to filter easily later.

## 3. Managing Wallets & Accounts
You don't just keep money in one place. ExpenseFlow lets you track your Cash, Bank Accounts, and Credit Cards separately.
* **Purpose:** Know exactly how much money is in each specific account.
* **How to use it:**
  1. Open the side menu (☰) and tap **Wallets & Accounts**.
  2. Tap **"+ New Wallet"** to add an account (e.g., "Chase Bank" or "Travel Fund").
  3. Enter a starting balance.
  4. Now, whenever you add a transaction, you can select which Wallet the money was taken from or added to. The app automatically calculates the live balance of every wallet!

## 4. Setting Savings Goals
Whether you are saving for a new laptop or a vacation, ExpenseFlow helps you visualize your progress.
* **Purpose:** Stay motivated by tracking money saved towards specific targets.
* **How to use it:**
  1. Go to **Savings Goals** in the menu.
  2. Tap **"Create Goal"**, enter a name (e.g., "New iPhone"), the target amount, and your deadline.
  3. As you save money, tap the **"+ Add Funds"** button on the goal card to log your deposits. The progress bar will fill up beautifully as you get closer to your target!

---

## 5. Setting Budgets
Stop overspending by setting monthly limits on specific categories.
* **Purpose:** Prevents you from spending too much on things like Shopping or Dining Out.
* **How to use it:**
  1. Open the **Budget** tab.
  2. Tap **"Set Budget"** and pick a category (e.g., "Food 🍔").
  3. Enter your maximum monthly limit.
  4. The app will now track how much you spend in that category. If you get close to the limit, the bar turns Orange. If you overspend, it turns Red and warns you!

---

## 6. Analytics & Insights
ExpenseFlow analyzes your spending locally (without ever sending your data to the cloud) to give you smart advice.
* **Purpose:** Understand your spending habits visually.
* **How to use it:**
  1. Go to the **Analytics** tab.
  2. Here you will see beautiful interactive charts: 
     * **Doughnut Charts** showing Income vs Expenses.
     * **Bar Charts** comparing your last 6 months of spending.
  3. Scroll to the bottom to see **AI Insights**. The app will warn you if a specific wallet is getting low, or if one purchase took up an unusually large chunk of your monthly budget!

---

## 7. Calendar & Reports
Need to see exactly what you spent on a specific day? Or need to export your data for taxes?
* **Calendar:** Go to the Calendar tab. Dates with transactions are highlighted. Tap any date to see exactly what you bought that day.
* **Reports:** Go to the Reports tab. You can filter transactions by "This Week," "This Month," or a "Custom Range." 
* **Exporting Data:** In the Reports tab, tap **Export CSV** (for Excel/Google Sheets) or **Export JSON** to safely backup your data to your phone's storage.

---

## 8. Security & Settings
Personalize your app and keep your financial data safe from prying eyes.
* **How to use it:** Open the **Settings** tab.
  * **Themes:** Switch between Light and Dark mode, or choose a Premium Accent color (like Royal Velvet or Emerald Forest) to change the look of the entire app.
  * **PIN Security:** Tap **"Setup App PIN"**. Enter a 4-digit code. Now, whenever the app is opened, it will require a keypad unlock, keeping your finances strictly private!
  * **Data Backup:** Because ExpenseFlow is 100% offline, your data stays on your phone. Tap **"Backup Data"** to download a save file. You can use **"Restore Data"** on a new device to instantly get all your transactions and settings back!
